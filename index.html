About

Hello! My name is Erik Stacy and I am the owner of Dark Lord Recordings. When I'm not working in the studio, I write and perform in my band Altered Perceptions, signed by Artery Recordings.

As a fellow musician, I know that all artists have a very clear vision for their music. You have spent endless hours crafting your songs and now it's time to make them stand out. Whether it is recording, mixing, or mastering, your satisfaction is my number one priority. That is why I will not stop until you are 100% satisfied with the final product.
